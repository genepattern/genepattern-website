Introduction

Combines Amaretto modules from multiple runs

Algorithm

“Community-AMARETTO” is used to integrate multiple regulatory networks to learn communities or subnetworks across them as was done in the pan-cancer (TCGA) AMARETTO paper. i.e., hypergeometric test for pairwise module overlap analysis, followed by the Girvan-Newman community or subnetwork detection algorithm.

 

References

Champion, Magali, Kevin Brennan, Tom Croonenborghs, Andrew J. Gentles, Nathalie Pochet, and Olivier Gevaert. “Module Analysis Captures Pancancer Genetically and Epigenetically Deregulated Cancer Driver Genes for Smoking and Antiviral Response.” EBioMedicine 27 (January 2018): 156–66. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ebiom.2017.11.028.
