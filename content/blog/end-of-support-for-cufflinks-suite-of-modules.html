The Cufflinks suite of modules is no longer under active development and is therefore deprecated. 

The Cufflinks suite of modules includes:

  • Cufflinks
  • Cuffcompare
  • Cuffmerge
  • Cuffdiff
  • CufflinksCuffmergePipeline (and the CufflinksWrapperPipeline)

It is no longer available on any of our servers or module repositories.

The module zip files and documentation are now archived in GitHub.

Please contact us if you have any questions.
