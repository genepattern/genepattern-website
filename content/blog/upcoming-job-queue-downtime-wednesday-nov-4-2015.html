On Wednesday, November 4, 2015 the GenePattern Broad-hosted servers will be down for scheduled system maintenance from 6am to 2pm EST (GMT -5).

The Broad-hosted GenePattern servers are:

  • genepattern.broadinstitute.org
  • gpbroad.broadinstitute.org (internal, requires Broad VPN access)

To facilitate a quick recovery from this downtime, we suggest that you refrain from submitting long-running jobs (4+ hours) after 5pm EST (GMT -5) today, Monday, November 2, 2015. 

After 5pm EST (GMT -5) tomorrow, Tuesday, November 3, 2015 job submission will be turned off. We have everything back up and running as close to 2pm EST (GMT -5) on Wednesday, November 4, as possible.

 

We will update this post, our Twitter feed, and the servers' system messages when the maintenance is complete. 

 

Nov 8 - Update: The system maintenance has completed successfully and the server has resumed normal function.
