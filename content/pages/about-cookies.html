Cookies are small strings of text that a website stores on your device when you browse the internet. They are created when your browser loads a particular website, and the site sends information to your browser for storage. Cookies allow the site to present you with information customized to fit your needs.

When you sign in to GenePattern, session cookies are created that allows the website to determine that you've logged in successfully, the name of the account at you've signed into and the state of your GenePattern home page. This allows GenePattern to present you with the jobs, pipelines and files associated with that account.

We do not store any other information in cookies, nor do we share that data with any third-parties.
